Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Information We Collect

Sentinel is a desktop application that processes your files locally on your device. We collect minimal data necessary to provide our service:

Account Information: When you sign in, we collect your email address and basic profile information from your authentication provider.

Usage Data: We track aggregate usage metrics to improve our service, such as feature usage counts. We do not store the content of your files.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to provide and improve Sentinel, manage your account, and communicate with you about updates and changes to our service.

Data Storage

Your files remain on your local device. We do not upload or store your files on our servers. AI processing is done via secure API calls where file content is not retained.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at privacy@app-sentinel.dev.
